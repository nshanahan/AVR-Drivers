Overview
========

 - Driver for Dallas Semiconductors One-Wire Interface (OWI).
 - Does not rely on UART hardware. Bit-level transactions are
   "bit-banged" (implemented as inline functions).
 - Developed for AVR family of microcontrollers.
 - Developed in accordance with AVR318: Dallas 1-Wire Master text.
    * http://www.atmel.com/images/doc2579.pdf
